Yorushika(ヨルシカ) consists of suis and n-buna at 2017. Because they don't want to give music preconceptions, they don't show more informations and their faces.

"Yorushika" is taken from a lyric in their song "Kumo to Yūrei": "Yoru shika mō nemurezu ni"(meaning "I can only sleep at night") from their first album Natsukusa ga Jyama wo suru.

On June 14, 2019, they debuted with issuing a single "Kokoro ni ana ga aita".
